Swimming is the latest sport that I picked up 4 years ago.
I am big fan of doing exercise. Growing up in Indonesia, badminton was my first sport. I trained in competitive badminton club. The expectation was high even for a 12 years old boy. As I grew, I started picking up other sports from volleyball, basketball, and running. Why swimming? I started getting injured more frequently like 7 - 8 years ago. At that time, I still played badminton and ran. Doctor suggested me stop all those activities and start picking up swimming. Initially, I was stubborn till one day I had minor tear on my achilles. That was the turning point and I started picking up a jammer to swim. Since then, I have not had any major injuries and a visit to a physiotherapist became thing in the past.
As I am creating this page, I have not been to go to the pool due to COVID-19 lockdown. The calmness and the feeling of the water while swimming are the things that I miss the most.
